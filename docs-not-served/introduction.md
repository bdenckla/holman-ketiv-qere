# Seventy-Seven (77) Selected Qere and Kethib Readings in Aleppo (A) and Leningrad (L) as Reflected in MAM and UXLC

The following list identifies 77 instances where the Miqra according to the Masorah (MAM) lacks an annotated note or indication of specific Qere and Kethib readings. While the MAM records most of these between the Aleppo and Leningrad codices, the examples below remain unaddressed. Notably, HaKeter lists five of these as Qere readings.

Although I considered reviewing Sassoon 1053, the current findings are sufficient to warrant including these in the MAM, either as marginal notes or as formal Qere/Kethib readings within the text.

## Breakdown of the 77 Readings

- 55 Instances: Aleppo contains a Masorah Circle, while Leningrad has a Qere reading.(Annotated as: A – Masorah Circle | L – Qere)
- 18 Instances: Aleppo has no Masorah Circle, but Leningrad has a Qere reading.(Annotated as: A – No Masorah Circle | L – Qere)
- 3 Instances: Both Aleppo and Leningrad contain a Qere reading.(Annotated as: A and L – Qere)
- 1 Instance: Uncertain Masorah Circle in Aleppo; Leningrad has a Qere reading.(Annotated as: A – Masorah Circle? | L – Qere)
